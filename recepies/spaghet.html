Spaghet

A guide to making a delicious plate of Spaghetti and meatballs that youll love

This classic Italian dish has sustained millions as Italian cusine has gained worldwide renown, use this guide to make this easy and delicious dish for any occasion

This recipe will make every italian grandma heap praise on your cooking skills.

Ingredients for meatballs

  • 1lb ground beef
  • 1/2 lb of groud pork
  • 1/2 cup of breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesean cheese
  • 1/4 cup of milk
  • 1/4 cup of finely chopped onion
  • 2 cloves of minced garlic
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tsp of dried orgeano
  • 1 tsp of dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Recpie for the Sauce

  • 1 can (28 ounces) of crushed tomaotes
  • 1 can (14 ounces) of diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup tomato paste
  • 1/4 cup of finely chopped onion
  • 2 cloves garlic minced
  • 2 tsp of dried basil
  • 2 tsp of dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Ingredients for the Pasta

  • 12 ounces of Spaghetti
  • Salt for the pasta water

Cooking Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Meatballs: in a bowl combine ground meat, crumbs, cheese, milk and spices, mix until well comnined, form into balls.
  2. Cook Meatballs: heat skillet over med heat and add olive oil, cook meatballs until all sides are browned, about 10-15 mins, set aside when done.
  3. Prepare the sauce: in the same skillet, add the onions and garlic, sautee until onion is translucent, then stir in the tomatoes and spices, let simmer for 20 mins.
  4. Cook the Pasta: bring pot of water to boil with salt, add spaghetti and cook til al dente, drain pasta and set aside.
  5. Combine: add the meatballs to the sauce, add a bit of pasta water to the sauce before draining, add your spagehtti and mix.
  6. Garnish and Serve: add fresh basil and parmesan cheese to taste.
