a cant miss recipe for making the perfect steak and nutritious Broccoli

the only recepie you need to make the perfect steak every time.

this is a surprisingly fast and healthy meal packed with protein and vitamins, youll impress all your guests with perfect consistent steaks

Ingredients List

  • 8-10 Oz cut of preferred steak cut, for this recipe we will be using a rib eye cut
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1/4 stick of butter
  • 1 Tbsp of Ghee
  • 1 trimmed head of Broccoli
  • 2 tsp of soy sauce
  • salt, pepper, and any other steak seasonsings you enjoy

Instructions for cooking

  1. Put 1 tsp of soy sauce to each side of the steak, rub it in well
  2. Season each side of the steak, rub in the seasoning until even and generous.
  3. De clove the garlic, and crush with the side of your knife
  4. Preheat your skillet for 2 minutes
  5. Add the ghee, wait until it is melted and small bubbles are forming
  6. Once ghee is hot, place your steak in, place it moving away from you.
  7. For different cooking temps;
  8. - Rare -> 130 degrees; soft feeling all over the steak when pressing down
  9. - Medium Rare -> 140 degrees; firm around the edges with a soft center
  10. - Medium Well -> 150 degrees; firm around with the center firming and the steak shrinking as the marbeling liqiuefies.
  11. - well done -> 160 degrees, firm all around, please do not eat steak like this as your ancestors will be ashamed!
  12. Cook to desired temperature on each side
  13. Once the steak is flipped, put in the butter and the crushed garlic into the pan.
  14. Take a teaspoon and baste the steak with the butter and garlic as the opposing side is being cooked
  15. Once the desired temperature of the opposing side is reached, remove the steak from the pan and place it onto your dish, leave it to rest for 5-7 minutes
  16. With the steak grease in the pan, place in your chopped and cleaned broccoli and mix in, add more soy sauce if needed to taste
  17. Add 1/4 cup of water into pan carefully, and lower heat to low, and place the top on the pan, leave it for 3 minutes.
  18. Place broccoli onto dish once time is up and serve.
