Recipe for Bacon and Egg breakfast

a step by step instructions for making a hearty breakfast

a breakfast classic, quick, full of protein and dense calories you need to take on the day

Ingredients list:

  • 3 whole eggs, add more if you are hungry.
  • 4-5 slices of Applewood smoked bacon, please feel free to use alterantives for health or religous reasons
  • 2 slices of bread, any kind
  • 1/4 tspn of butter

Instructions for cooking

  1. Preheat skillet on medium heat for 2 mins
  2. Place bacon strips in skillet, fry each side until golden brown
  3. Place cooked bacon onto paper towel on a plate and set aside
  4. with the bacon grease still in the pan, crack in your eggs and let them fry for 2-3 mins
  5. if you wish to scramble the eggs, just mix them before in a bowl and add it to the skillet
  6. while the eggs are cooking, throw your bread into the toaster and toast to desired color.
  7. Butter your toast, place all food onto the plate and serve
  8. add salt and pepper to taste, add any favorite hot sauces to eggs to extra kick.
